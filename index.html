    How a project runs

    From idea to launch.

    Every project follows a clear process to ensure quality, performance, and a successful launch.

    01

    Discovery

    We learn about your business, goals, and target audience to understand exactly what your website needs to achieve.

    02

    Planning

    We define the website structure, user journey, and content strategy before development begins.

    03
